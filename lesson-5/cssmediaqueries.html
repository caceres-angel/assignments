The City

Salta is the capital of the Salta Province in Argentina. The city can be divided in two parts, the old town and the modern city.

What?

Salta is a city on the province of Salta.

Where?

Salta is a Province in the the Country of Argentina.

How?

You can reach Salta by taking buses and flights from all over Argentina.
